# InstinctShield AI

## AI-Powered Real-Time Scam & Deepfake Call Detection System

InstinctShield AI is a multimodal AI system designed to detect AI-generated voice scams and conversational fraud patterns before financial damage occurs.

Unlike traditional spam detection systems that rely on number-based databases, InstinctShield AI analyzes:

- Voice authenticity (Real vs Synthetic)
- Conversational scam intent
- Psychological manipulation patterns
- OTP extraction tactics

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

AI-powered voice cloning scams and deepfake fraud are rapidly increasing across digital financial ecosystems.

Modern scam techniques include:

- AI-generated voice impersonation
- Caller ID spoofing
- New SIM-based fraud
- Urgency and fear manipulation
- OTP extraction attacks

Existing spam detection tools fail to detect first-time or spoofed numbers and cannot analyze voice authenticity or contextual fraud intent.

InstinctShield AI addresses this gap.
